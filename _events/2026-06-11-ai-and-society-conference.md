A practical, hands-on workshop for economists and social scientists who want to use AI tools responsibly and reproducibly in their research workflows.

We start with the technical foundations of chat completion, then move through agentic coding practice, project structure, skill authoring, verification workflows, and recognizing data processing errors. No slideshow — this is collaborative work: live demos, guided exercises, and patterns you can apply to your own research projects right away.

The workshop is in English so materials can be reused later. No prior AI experience required. AI access is provided, but bring your own laptop. Detailed technical instructions will be sent the day before the event.
